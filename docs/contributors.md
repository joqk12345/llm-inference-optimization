## Contribution Types

We recognize contributions in many forms:

### 📝 Content Contributions
- Fixing typos and errors
- Improving explanations
- Adding examples
- Writing success stories

### 💻 Code Contributions
- Bug fixes
- New features
- Performance improvements
- Test cases

### 💬 Community Contributions
- Answering questions
- Helping in Discord
- Moderating discussions
- Organizing events

### 📢 Promotion Contributions
- Sharing on social media
- Blogging about the project
- Telling friends and colleagues

## How to Get Recognized

### Monthly Recognition (Top 10)
Each month, the top 10 contributors receive:
- ✅ Free Pro membership for 1 month
- ✅ Mentioned in monthly update
- ✅ Special Discord role
- ✅ Your contribution highlighted

### Quarterly Recognition (Top 3)
Each quarter, the top 3 contributors receive:
- ✅ Free 1-hour consulting session
- ✅ Featured in "Contributor Spotlight"
- ✅ Priority for feature requests
- ✅ Permanent badge on your profile

### Annual Recognition
At the end of each year:
- ✅ Your name in the book acknowledgments
- ✅ Lifetime Pro membership
- ✅ Special contributor badge
- ✅ Invitation to annual contributor event
